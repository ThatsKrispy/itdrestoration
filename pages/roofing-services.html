Why ITD for Roofing?

Most restoration companies sub out roofing work. ITD handles it in-house with our own licensed contractors. That means one point of contact for your entire claim, coordinated scheduling between interior and exterior work, and no finger-pointing between contractors on scope or responsibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Florida homeowner's insurance typically covers sudden, accidental roof damage from storms, hail, and wind. Coverage for wear and age-related deterioration varies by policy and insurer. Florida's insurance market has been volatile — many policies now have wind/hail deductibles or limited roof coverage. We help document storm-caused damage and work with your adjuster to maximize your covered claim.

We provide 24/7 emergency tarping and aim to be on-site within 2–4 hours of your call. During major storm events, response times may be longer due to volume — call us early. Emergency tarping protects your interior from further damage while permanent repairs are planned and scheduled.

A typical residential roof replacement takes 1–3 days for the roofing work itself. Insurance approval, material ordering, and permitting can add 2–6 weeks to the overall timeline. We coordinate permit pulling and inspections and keep you informed throughout the process.
