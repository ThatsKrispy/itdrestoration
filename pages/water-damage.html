Water Damage

When Water Strikes, We're Already on the Way

Water damage doesn't wait. Whether it's a burst pipe in the middle of the night, a slow leak that became a flood, or a South Florida storm that overwhelmed your drainage — every hour that passes makes the damage worse and the restoration more costly.

At ITD Restoration, our certified technicians are trained in the latest IICRC water damage restoration protocols. We arrive quickly, assess comprehensively, and restore thoroughly — from initial extraction to the final coat of paint.

Common Causes We Handle

Burst or Leaking Pipes

Supply line failures, frozen pipes, and plumbing ruptures

Roof Leaks & Storm Water

Heavy rain intrusion and hurricane-related water damage

Appliance Failures

Washing machines, dishwashers, water heaters, and AC units

Flooding

Ground water intrusion, flash floods, and sewage backup

Our Restoration Process

1

Emergency Response & Assessment

We arrive fast with moisture meters, thermal cameras, and a full team. We identify the water source, classify the damage, and create a detailed remediation plan before touching anything.

2

Water Extraction

Industrial truck-mounted extractors remove standing water from all affected areas — floors, walls, subfloors, and crawl spaces — rapidly and completely.

3

Drying & Dehumidification

We place high-velocity air movers and LGR dehumidifiers throughout the structure. Moisture readings are taken daily until all materials reach acceptable drying goals.

4

Antimicrobial Treatment

We treat all affected surfaces with EPA-registered antimicrobial agents to stop mold growth before it starts — especially critical in South Florida's humid climate.

5

Restoration & Rebuilding

Once dry and clean, we restore or replace damaged drywall, flooring, cabinetry, and any structural elements — bringing your property back to pre-loss condition.
