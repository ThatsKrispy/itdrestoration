Biohazard Remediation

Professional, Compliant, and Handled With Care

Biohazard situations require specialized training, equipment, and disposal protocols that go far beyond standard cleaning services. Attempting to address biohazardous materials without proper PPE and training puts individuals at serious risk of exposure to bloodborne pathogens, infectious diseases, and chemical hazards.

ITD Restoration's certified biohazard technicians respond with discretion, compassion, and the professionalism these situations require. We follow all OSHA, EPA, and Florida Department of Health regulations for biohazard remediation and waste disposal.

Situations We Handle

Trauma & Crime Scene

Blood and bodily fluid cleanup following accidents, injuries, or crimes

Unattended Death

Sensitive, thorough cleanup and decontamination

Drug Lab Cleanup
