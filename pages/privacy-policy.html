Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website form, call our phone number, or otherwise communicate with ITD Restoration, we may collect: your name, email address, phone number, property address, and details about the service you are requesting. We do not collect payment information through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule service, coordinate with your insurance company on your behalf, and follow up on completed jobs. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Analytics

We may use anonymized, aggregate analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website in order to improve it. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information you share with us. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information online.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information that we hold. To make such a request, contact us at info@itdrestoration.com or call (866) 219-9059.
