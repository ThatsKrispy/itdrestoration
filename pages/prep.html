P.R.E.P. Program

Be Ready Before the Disaster — Not After

In a restoration emergency, the first 24–48 hours determine how much damage your property sustains and how smoothly your insurance claim goes. P.R.E.P. clients are positioned to start the recovery process hours faster than non-enrolled properties.

When you enroll in P.R.E.P., ITD visits your property, documents every room and system, identifies potential vulnerability points, and creates a customized emergency response plan. That file is on file with our dispatch team — so when you call, we already know your property.

Priority Dispatch

P.R.E.P. clients go to the front of the queue during high-demand events like hurricanes.

Pre-Documented Property

Photos, floor plans, and system locations on file — supporting faster insurance documentation.

Custom Response Plan

A property-specific plan identifying shutoffs, access points, and priority areas.

Insurance Coordination

Pre-documented baseline speeds claims filing and reduces disputes over pre-existing conditions.
