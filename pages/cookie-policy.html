Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

Cookies & Storage We Use

The ITD Restoration website uses only what is necessary for the basic functioning of the site, plus optional categories that load only with your consent. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies.

NecessarySecurity, navigation, and remembering your cookie and accessibility preferences (stored locally in your browser)Always on

Managing Cookies

When you first visit, a consent banner lets you accept all, reject optional, or customize categories individually. You can change your choices at any time using the control (also in the footer of every page). You can also control and delete cookies through your browser settings — disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

Third-Party Services

This website embeds a Google Maps iframe for displaying our service area. The map loads only after you allow the "Maps & embeds" category; until then a placeholder is shown and no request is made to Google. Google may set cookies through this embed in accordance with their own cookie policy. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for details.

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at info@itdrestoration.com.
